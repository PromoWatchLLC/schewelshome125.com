1.
PROMOTION DESCRIPTION. The Schewels Home 125th Anniversary Giveaway (the “Sweepstakes”) begins on 4/19/22 and ends
on 5/11/22 (“Promotion Period”). The Sweepstakes consists of Weekly Sweepstakes periods (the “Weekly Sweepstakes”) and a
Grand Prize Sweepstakes (the “Grand Prize Sweepstakes”). Each Weekly Sweepstakes will consist of a Weekly Entry Period (“Weekly
Entry Period”) to receive entries each Week of the Promotion Period. The Sweepstakes provides eligible entrants with the opportunity to
visit a participating Schewels Home store in the states of North Carolina, Virginia and West Virginia, look for the in-store Sweepstakes
display and complete an official entry found at the display in order to enter. For complete list of participating stores, go online to
www.schewelshome.com/stores/.
There will be one (1) Weekly Sweepstakes winner for each participating store in the Sweepstakes for each Week of the Promotion
Period. All eligible Weekly entries from all participating stores will automatically be submitted into the Grand Prize Sweepstakes; where
one (1) Grand Prize winner will be randomly drawn at the conclusion of the Promotion Period in the Grand Prize Sweepstakes. The
details of the Weekly Entry Period, the Weekly Sweepstakes and the Grand Prize Sweepstakes are described in further detail below. By
participating in the Sweepstakes, each entrant unconditionally accepts and agrees to comply with and abide by these Official Rules and
the decisions of Schewel Furniture Company (“Sponsor”), which shall be final and binding in all respects. Entry in the Sweepstakes
does not constitute entry into any other promotion, contest or sweepstakes. For purposes of this Sweepstakes, a “Weekly Entry Period”
is defined as a consecutive seven (7) day period of time during the Promotion Period starting on a Tuesday and ending seven (7) days
later on a Monday.
2.
ELIGIBILITY. Open to legal residents of the states of Maryland, North Carolina, Pennsylvania, Tennessee, Virginia and West Virginia

regulations apply. Void where prohibited or restricted by law.
3.

form found at the in-store Sweepstakes display to enter the Sweepstakes. Weekly Sweepstakes entries will automatically be entered into
the Grand Prize Sweepstakes. Limit one (1) Weekly Sweepstakes entry per person, per Week. All entries in excess of this limit will
be deemed void. Sponsor is not responsible for lost, late, misdirected, illegible, incomplete, damaged or mutilated entry forms. No
mechanically reproduced, altered, forged, or facsimile entries will be accepted. All entries become the property of Sponsor and will not
be returned. Sponsor is not responsible for any change of mailing address, email address and/or telephone number of entrants.

No more than the number of prizes set forth in these Official Rules will be awarded. Use of any automated system to participate is

4.
RANDOM DRAWING. One (1) Weekly Sweepstakes winner for each participating store for each Weekly Entry Period will be selected in a
random drawing on the drawing date as noted in the chart in rule #3 from all eligible Weekly Sweepstakes entries received (for each
participating store) by store management personnel of the Sponsor or its designee. At the end of the Promotion Period, one (1) Grand Prize
Sweepstakes winner will be selected in a random drawing on or about 5/17/22 from all eligible Weekly Sweepstakes entries received by all
participating stores by Sponsor or its designee. Subject to verification of eligibility and compliance with these Official Rules, the potential
winners will be declared the official winners of the Sweepstakes. The decisions of the Sponsor are final and binding.
5.
WINNER NOTIFICATION. Each potential prize winner will be notified by email and/or phone by the store management personnel where their
entry was submitted, using contact information provided or collected at the time of entry. Sponsor shall have no liability for any winner
notification that is lost, intercepted or not received by a potential winner for any reason. If, despite reasonable efforts, a potential winner does
not respond within seventy-two (72) hours of the first notification attempt, or if the prize or prize notification is returned as unclaimed or
undeliverable to such potential winner, such potential winner will forfeit his or her prize and an alternate winner may be selected. Sponsor, in
its sole discretion, will attempt to contact up to two (2) potential winners of a prize in accordance with the above procedure, after which the
prize in question may go unawarded if it remains unclaimed. If any potential prize winner is found to be ineligible, or if he or she has not
complied with these Official Rules or declines the prize for any reason prior to award, such prize will be forfeited and an alternate prize winner
may be selected. Potential prize winners will be provided with the Winner’s Documents for their completion and return to the Sponsor in order
to claim their prize as more fully described in rule #7.
6.
PRIZES/APPROXIMATE RETAIL VALUES (ARV)/ODDS. One (1) Grand Prize is available in the Grand Prize Sweepstakes. Grand Prize
winner will receive a $10,000 in furniture from Schewels Home. Winner will have the opportunity select the furniture of their choice from any
furniture offered at the Schewels Home store where they submitted their entry; up to $10,000 (including applicable taxes). ARV of Grand
Prize is $10.000. One Hundred Fifty (150) Weekly Prizes (one (1) Weekly Sweepstakes Prize per store each Weekly Entry Period x 50
stores). Each Weekly Sweepstakes Prize winner will receive a store-selected piece of furniture or home accessory from the Schewels Home
store where they submitted their entry. ARV range of each Weekly Sweepstakes Prize is minimum of $599 - up to $999 (ARV depends on
item selected by participating store). Weekly Sweepstakes Prize item to be awarded is at the sole discretion of the Sponsor and its
participating stores. Total ARV of all prizes up to $159,850. Odds of winning a Weekly Sweepstakes Prize depend on total number of eligible
entries received each Week at each participating store. Odds of winning the Grand Prize Sweepstakes depends on total number of eligible
entries received during the Promotion Period all participating stores. Unclaimed prizes will not be awarded. All applicable federal, state and
local taxes on prizes are the sole responsibility of the prize winners. Limit one (1) Weekly Sweepstakes Prize and the Grand Prize per
household.
