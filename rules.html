SCHEWELS HOME 125TH ANNIVERSARY GIVEAWAY

OFFICIAL RULES

NO PURCHASE NECESSARY TO ENTER OR WIN. A PURCHASE WILL NOT INCREASE YOUR CHANCES OF WINNING. VOID WHERE PROHIBITED.

1. PROMOTION DESCRIPTION. The Schewels Home 125th Anniversary Giveaway (the “Sweepstakes”) begins on 4/19/22 and ends on 5/11/22 (“Promotion Period”). The Sweepstakes consists of Weekly Sweepstakes periods (the “Weekly Sweepstakes”) and a Grand Prize Sweepstakes (the “Grand Prize Sweepstakes”). Each Weekly Sweepstakes will consist of a Weekly Entry Period (“Weekly Entry Period”) to receive entries each Week of the Promotion Period. The Sweepstakes provides eligible entrants with the opportunity to visit a participating Schewels Home store in the states of North Carolina, Virginia and West Virginia, look for the in-store Sweepstakes display and complete an official entry found at the display in order to enter. For complete list of participating stores, go online to www.schewelshome.com/stores/.

There will be one (1) Weekly Sweepstakes winner for each participating store in the Sweepstakes for each Week of the Promotion Period. All eligible Weekly entries from all participating stores will automatically be submitted into the Grand Prize Sweepstakes; where one (1) Grand Prize winner will be randomly drawn at the conclusion of the Promotion Period in the Grand Prize Sweepstakes. The details of the Weekly Entry Period, the Weekly Sweepstakes and the Grand Prize Sweepstakes are described in further detail below. By participating in the Sweepstakes, each entrant unconditionally accepts and agrees to comply with and abide by these Official Rules and the decisions of Schewel Furniture Company (“Sponsor”), which shall be final and binding in all respects. Entry in the Sweepstakes does not constitute entry into any other promotion, contest or sweepstakes. For purposes of this Sweepstakes, a “Weekly Entry Period” is defined as a consecutive seven (7) day period of time during the Promotion Period starting on a Tuesday and ending seven (7) days later on a Monday.

2. ELIGIBILITY. Open to legal residents of the states of Maryland, North Carolina, Pennsylvania, Tennessee, Virginia and West Virginia who are eighteen (18) years of age or older at the time of entry. Employees of Schewel Furniture Company, its parent company, subsidiaries, affiliates, partners, advertising, promotion and other agencies (collectively, "Promotion Entities") and their immediate family members and/or those living in the same household of each are not eligible to participate. All federal, state and local laws and regulations apply. Void where prohibited or restricted by law.

3. HOW TO ENTER. Visit a participating Schewels Home store in North Carolina, Virginia or West Virginia and complete an official entry form found at the in-store Sweepstakes display to enter the Sweepstakes. Weekly Sweepstakes entries will automatically be entered into the Grand Prize Sweepstakes. Limit one (1) Weekly Sweepstakes entry per person, per Week. All entries in excess of this limit will be deemed void. Sponsor is not responsible for lost, late, misdirected, illegible, incomplete, damaged or mutilated entry forms. No mechanically reproduced, altered, forged, or facsimile entries will be accepted. All entries become the property of Sponsor and will not be returned. Sponsor is not responsible for any change of mailing address, email address and/or telephone number of entrants.

Weekly Sweepstakes entry periods and drawing dates are as follows:

Week # Start Weekly Entry Period End Weekly Entry Period Weekly Drawing Date
1 4/19/22 4/25/22 4/26/22
2 4/26/22 5/2/22 5/3/22
3 5/3/22 5/11/22 5/12/22

Non-winning entries in the Weekly Sweepstakes will automatically rollover into any subsequent Weekly drawings. Only fully completed entries are eligible.

No more than the number of prizes set forth in these Official Rules will be awarded. Use of any automated system to participate is prohibited and will result in disqualification. All potential winning entries are subject to verification by Sponsor, whose decisions are final and binding. A participant is not a winner of any prize, unless and until said participant’s eligibility has been verified and said participant has been notified that verification is complete.

4. RANDOM DRAWING. One (1) Weekly Sweepstakes winner for each participating store for each Weekly Entry Period will be selected in a random drawing on the drawing date as noted in the chart in rule #3 from all eligible Weekly Sweepstakes entries received (for each participating store) by store management personnel of the Sponsor or its designee. At the end of the Promotion Period, one (1) Grand Prize Sweepstakes winner will be selected in a random drawing on or about 5/17/22 from all eligible Weekly Sweepstakes entries received by all participating stores by Sponsor or its designee. Subject to verification of eligibility and compliance with these Official Rules, the potential winners will be declared the official winners of the Sweepstakes. The decisions of the Sponsor are final and binding.

6. PRIZES/APPROXIMATE RETAIL VALUES (ARV)/ODDS. One (1) Grand Prize is available in the Grand Prize Sweepstakes. Grand Prize winner will receive a $10,000 in furniture from Schewels Home. Winner will have the opportunity select the furniture of their choice from any furniture offered at the Schewels Home store where they submitted their entry; up to $10,000 (including applicable taxes). ARV of Grand Prize is $10.000. One Hundred Fifty (150) Weekly Prizes (one (1) Weekly Sweepstakes Prize per store each Weekly Entry Period x 50 stores). Each Weekly Sweepstakes Prize winner will receive a store-selected piece of furniture or home accessory from the Schewels Home store where they submitted their entry. ARV range of each Weekly Sweepstakes Prize is minimum of $599 – up to $999 (ARV depends on item selected by participating store). Weekly Sweepstakes Prize item to be awarded is at the sole discretion of the Sponsor and its participating stores. Total ARV of all prizes up to $159,850. Odds of winning a Weekly Sweepstakes Prize depend on total number of eligible entries received each Week at each participating store. Odds of winning the Grand Prize Sweepstakes depends on total number of eligible entries received during the Promotion Period all participating stores. Unclaimed prizes will not be awarded. All applicable federal, state and local taxes on prizes are the sole responsibility of the prize winners. Limit one (1) Weekly Sweepstakes Prize and the Grand Prize per household.
